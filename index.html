About C2

Daily Life: What we do; how we be.

Conner 2 takes the best parts of a Family, Dorm, and even Fraternity, and combines them into something we like to call ΕΝΓ, for English House.

Want some company while you work? Feel free to drop by the 23 almost any time of day or night, where we together work on PSets, listen to music, and even eat occasional free food provided by the chefy C2 community.

If ever you need a break from the PSets (read: always), the 23 also provides with a plethora of fun board games, and a host of people who would love to drop what they're doing for a good round of Dominion, Star Realms, 7 Wonders, Settlers of Catan, or whatever else you can find in our box of board/card‑based wonders.

One big thing that sets C2 apart form other dorms is our big/little system, where each freshman is put into a 'family line' that usually has three or more members spread out across classes each year. Personally, I always turned to my big² first when I was having issues freshman year be it academic, athletic, or just dealing with any of the many other stresses brought about by life at the Institvte.
While other family lines certainly need not be as close, it's nice to know that between your floormates, your family line, the Floor Chairs, and the Floor Mom & Dad, there's always someone there to talk to.

And of course, what Fa-orm-ternity would be complete without the occasional party? At least once a month we have our own parties, where C2 plus posse get together and have good times yielding such testimonals as "Huh, I didin't know C2 knew how to party" from our cross-campus friends.

Long Term: Events and Affairs

  • September

    Freshmen Get Fed

    Traditionally, after FLEX, the entire floor goes out to dine at a thai place called Chili Duck. It's a great way to meet your new floor, and get some C2-Provided good food while you're at it. Unfortunaly, Chili Duck recently got shut down due to a laundry list of health code violations. So we'll figure something else out.

  • November

    Apples Get Baked

    Enjoy cooking? Enjoy being better than other people? C2 enjoys both. That's why every year at the Burton Conner Apple Bake, we win. I slept through last year's, so I dont have much to write about it. But people tell me it was lit.

  • December

    Gifts Get Given

    Conner 2 Non-Denominational Gift Giving Day!
    We all gather around a seasonal item and give gifts to each other in secret. Also, we eat more free food that has historically been provided by Chili Duck... so we'll see how that goes.

  • May

    Trails Get Hiked

    Postfinal Conner 2 Cabin Tripping! Join us in a lovely New Hampshire cabin, where we play games, eat food, hike, and do all the good things accociated with school being over.
